Stremio Guide

The Ultimate Stremio Setup

Everything you need to know about setting up Stremio with Addons and Debrid services for a perfect streaming experience.

Step 1: Account & Installation

Before starting, create a free account at Stremio.com. Creating an account lets you sync your progress, library, and installed addons across all your devices seamlessly.

Tip: Always set up Stremio initially on a PC, Mac, or Android phone. Once configured, you can just log into your TV (Android TV, Samsung, LG) and all your setup will instantly sync.
Windows macOS Android

Step 2: Optimal Settings

Once installed and logged in, open the Settings gear icon.

  • Enable Hardware Accelerated Decoding to ensure smooth playback and prevent crashing.
  • If you plan to stream purely via torrents (without a Debrid service), set the Torrent Profile to Ultra Fast and the cache size to 10GB or Infinite.

Step 3: What is a Debrid Service?

A Debrid service acts as a premium middleman between you and torrents. Instead of you relying on peers to download chunks of a file, the Debrid service downloads the torrent instantly to their high-speed servers.

Benefits of Debrid:

No Buffering

Stream massive 4K REMUX files securely over HTTPS at maximum ISP speed.

No VPN Required

You stream via standard HTTPS direct from the service, hiding P2P activity from ISPs.

Recommended Services: Real-Debrid (Most popular, €3/mo), TorBox, or Premiumize.

Step 4: Installing Addons

1. Remove Default Addons

Go to the Addons menu (puzzle icon). Uninstall WatchHub and Public Domain Movies to declutter your streams.

2. Install Torrentio (The Main Provider)

Torrentio is the core addon that scrapes the web for content links.

  1. Visit torrentio.strem.fun on the device where Stremio is installed.
  2. (Optional) If you have a Debrid service, select your provider from the dropdown.
  3. Enter your API Token from your Debrid provider (found in your provider's account settings).
  4. Click Install. This will prompt Stremio to open and install the addon.

3. Install Cyberflix & TMDB (For Catalogues)

To make your homepage look like Netflix with categories like "Trending, New on Prime, New on Disney+", you need Metadata addons.

  • Cyberflix creates beautiful lists of platforms.
  • Cinemeta (already installed but crucial to keep).
  • The Movie Database Addon for exceptional localized metadata.

You're All Set! 🎉

Search for any movie or series, and enjoy seamless playback!
